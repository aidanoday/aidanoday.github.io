👋 Hello! My name is

AIDAN O'DAY

Based in Seattle, WA, I am a seasoned UX designer, product manager, and startup veteran with robust experience in user research, interface design, and product leadership. I’m leveraging my unique background in human values, ontology, and social psychology and my interests in tech, industrial design, art, philosophy, and the creative world to gain detailed understandings of user needs, tackle complex problems, and create delightful products.

From 2019-2022 I was the lead designer and team lead for cross-platform digital products at ILWE.io, wearing lots of hats to save on haircuts. Over 36 months our small scrappy team raised a pre-seed round, brought to life a web app, and released two mobile apps aimed at empowering and connecting the next generation of 'bedroom creatives.' Now, I'm working as a freelance UX designer and product strategist helping other teams identify problems and develop meaningful solutions.

I'm looking to join a new team working to make life better for people, and I'm looking for industry veterans who can challenge and mentor me. Maybe you're looking for a new teammate? Feel free to poke around my portfolio, check out my resume, and reach out if anything catches your eye.
