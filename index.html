About: Based in Seattle, WA, I am a tinker artist turned product designer. Growing up, I took hard drives apart with my friends and siblings to make art with the pieces. Today, I bring that same technical and creative curiosity, an academic background in psychology and user-centered design, and seven years of product design experience to the table.

Most recently: I was working as a product designer on the Outbound Logistics Technology team at Lululemon; I designed tools to more easily automate logistics decisions from Dec 2024- Dec 2025.

Where I've Been:
I moved to Seattle and consulted as a freelance UX designer and product strategist. I helped the founders of Knomee refine, develop, and prototype their vision, which allowed them to raise an initial $250,000 pre-seed round. In July 2023, I joined inquirED as their first UX/UI designer, where I established a Figma design system using and brought better navigation, accessibility, and mobile compatibility to the forefront of their curriculum platform for teachers.

I was co-founder and product lead for cross-platform digital products at ILWE.io, wearing lots of hats to save on haircuts. Over 36 months our small scrappy team raised a $400,000 pre-seed round, brought to life a web app, and released two mobile apps aimed at empowering and connecting the next generation of 'bedroom creatives.'

Thank you for coming to poke around my portfolio! I built it myself in HTML, Java, and CSS. Stay tuned for updates, check out my resume, drop me a line with feedback and reccomendations. And don't hesitate to reach out if anything catches your eye.
